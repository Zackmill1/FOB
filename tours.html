Album Eras

  • From Under the Cork Tree

    From Under the Cork Tree was the band's first record where Patrick Stump created all the music and Pete Wentz wrote the lyrics. The album debuted and peaked at number nine on the Billboard 200 when it release in May 2005. The single, "Sugar, We're Goin' Down," reached number eight in the US Billboard Hot 100 and then in the UK chart in February 2006. The Second single, "Dance,Dance," was certified 3x Platinum in 2014.This has been the group's best selling ablum with over 2.7 million copies sold in the US.

    2005-2006

  • Infinity on High

    Infinity on High was the first depature Fall Out Boy made from their original sound. In the ablum's first week of release, it sold 260,000 copies and debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200. The third single released from this album, "Thnks fr th Mmrs," is one of the bands most popular songs and most recognizable as well.

    2007

  • Folie a Deux

    Folie a Deux or a madness shared by two, marked the beginning of problems for the band that would lead to their eventual hiatus. Trohman and Hurley felt that they did not have as much say in the band as Wentz and Stump. This album was very decisive among fans upon its release. The album debuted at number eight on the US Billboard 200 chart and sold 150,000 copies its first week. The band eventually decided on a hiatus and much needed break.

    2008-2009

  • Hiatus

    During the band's hiatus, the members worked on side projects and took time for themselves.Patrick stump release a solo album titled Soul Punk which he wrote, produced, and played every instrument for every track. Pete Wentz formed a duo with artist Bebe Rexha called Black Cards. Andy Hurley and Joe Trohman formed a metal band The Damned Things.

    2010-2012

  • Save Rock and Roll

    Wentz and Stump met after several years to catch up and brought up the first thoughts of coming back together as a band. After contacting Hurley and Trohman, the band went on the right their next album Save Rock and Roll. This album was meant to reinvent the groups sound from scratch. Save Rock and Roll debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart and sold 154,000 copies in its first week. The lead single, "My Songs Know What You Did in the Dark (Light Em Up)", peaked at number 13 on the Billboard Hot 100. It was certified 3x Platinum with over 3 million sales.

    2013-2014

  • American Beauty/American Psycho

    "Centuries" was the first single to be released from this album and premiered on September 8, 2014 on BBC Radio 1 and peaked at number ten on the Billboard Hot 100.The album was released on January 20, 2015 and Debuted at number 1 on the US Billboard 200 with 192,000 sales in its first week.

    2014-2016

  • Mania

    On April 27, 2017, the band announced their new album, Mania, for release on September 15 as well as its first single, "Young and Menace." The album was eventually pushed back to a January 19, 2018 release so that the band could perfect its sound. Mania debuted number one on the Billboard 200 making it teh band's consecutive and fourth chart-topping debut overall.

    2017-Present
